The EHSSU was formed in January 2023 with the aim of giving a voice to young people in Edinburgh.
As High School students, it is often difficult to have our voices heard. However by joining together,
we can collectively raise issues and create a better and more positive educational experience for all.

Membership is open to anyone who currently attends a Secondary School in Edinburgh.

Advantages of joining include:

  • Getting your voice heard and put pressure on those who can make changes
  • Collectively raising the issues that matter to you
  • Being part of a network of other students with common interests and goals
  • Helping work towards a better education experience for all
